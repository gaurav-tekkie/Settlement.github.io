Our Story

We believe that balancing financial relationships shouldn't require manual math, endless reminders, or awkward conversations.

The Problem We're Solving

Traditional expense splitting apps stop at the immediate group level. If you owe a friend, you have to settle directly with them. But human networks are vastly more connected than that. Often, the money you owe someone eventually routes to someone else entirely across a larger grid of people.

We saw an opportunity to make asset and debt matching infinitely smarter.

Our Mission

Settlement was built to create an automated network architecture for peer-to-peer liabilities. By applying network graph logic—the underlying structural concepts that make blockchain ledgers so efficient—we engineered an algorithm that scans systemic debt loops and optimizes them down to zero.

Trustless Systems

You don't need to know the entire chain to trust that the balance math is flawlessly accounted for.

Uncompromising Privacy

We protect your personal financial records by treating data as modular cryptographic blocks.
